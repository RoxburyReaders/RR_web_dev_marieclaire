First Church in Roxbury

First Church in Roxbury has been in continuous use since early English settlers built the first meetinghouse on this site in 1632.

http://www.uuum.org


Historic Boston

Historic Boston Incorporated (HBI) works with local partners to transform at-risk historic buildings in Boston’s neighborhoods for new sustainable uses.

http://historicboston.org/


Massachusetts Historical Society

Founded in 1791, the Massachusetts Historical Society is an invaluable resource for American history, life, and culture. Its extraordinary collections tell the story of America through millions of rare and unique documents, artifacts, and irreplaceable national treasures.

www.masshist.org/about


Museum of African American History

The Museum of African American History is New England largest museum dedicated to preserving, conserving and interpreting the contributions of African Americans.

http://maah.org/about.htm
